Frequently Asked Questions

What are your check-in/check-out times?

Check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Do you allow pets?

Yes, we are pet-friendly! We allow dogs up to 50 lbs in designated pet-friendly rooms. A pet fee of $50 per stay applies.

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary self-parking for all guests. Valet parking is also available for $25 per night.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 7 days before check-in. Cancellations within 7 days receive a 50% refund. No refund for no-shows.

Do you have wheelchair accessible rooms?

Yes, we have several ADA-compliant rooms with wheelchair accessibility. Please mention this requirement when booking.

Is breakfast included?

Breakfast is included for all guests staying in Deluxe rooms and Suites. Standard room guests can add breakfast for $25 per person.
